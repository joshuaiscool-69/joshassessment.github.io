About Us

Stop Cyberbullying is an awareness and education project dedicated to creating a safer and kinder online world. this web site is aimed to educate individuals, schools, and communities about the real effects of online harassment and how to prevent it.

What We Do

  • Raise awareness through education and community outreach
  • Share real stories from those affected by cyberbullying
  • Provide links to support hotlines and safe online spaces
  • Promote positive online behavior and digital respect
